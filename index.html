  • Out Loud

    Out Loud was a business idea, around which I made a project from scratch, under Mr. Nikit Phadke (+1 (650) 703-1447) who is an alumnus of University of Utah and is currently working as a React Native consultant in California. Essentially, Out Loud is a universal text to speech converter, which would convert any format of text file to any format of audio.

    The emphasis of this project was to learn how to take an idea and put it out into the real world. Hence, I did not actually create a text-to-speech converter, but engaged in putting it together as a business initiative. I began with creating a logo and tagline for my idea and after that I make a landing page for it. Later on, I was guided by Mr. Phadke to develop a react native application to promote Out Loud.

    This undertaking not only taught me how to get my idea together, but also taught me various new practices which helped me gain beneficial interdisciplinary skills.

  • Sambhav Design Studio

    Sambhav Design Studio is based in Indore city of Madhya Pradesh. It is a newly established design studio founded by a bunch of alumni of National Institute of Design (NID), the oldest and best design school in India. I did an identity branding project for this young establishment after knowing them closely.

    Essentially, I designed visiting card for one of the founders, Mr. Mohit Rajora (+91 97126 71329), letterhead and envelope for Sambhav Design Studio. Also, I was a part of creating tagline, posters and brochures for them.

    This project taught me how to connect with and deeply understand people and their thought process. It also helped me hone my software skills.
